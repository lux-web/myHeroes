Make your signature colorful

Hello Lux here, recently I found out that signature support html code! You can make your signature colorful, bold, italic, etc

Syntax

Font Color

<color=#ff0000>"Enter your text here"</color>

note: #ff0000 stands for color code. Each color have different codes

Color Picker

Font Size:

<size=69>"Enter your text here"</size>

note: <size=69> enter any number to your liking

Bold

<b>"Enter your text here"</b>

Italic

<i>"Enter your text here"</i>

Mix and Match

Mix and match above syntax to make cool signature! For example:

<size=69><color=#eedd82>Hello, I'm Lux!</color></size>
