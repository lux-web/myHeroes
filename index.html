MyHeroes:SEA

"My Heroes" is a classic pixel-style barrage shooting ARPG game that players can freely team up for adventure. The game is centered on the pursuit of refreshing and exciting barrage shooting battles, with a high degree of freedom and strategic Diablo-style building and development gameplay, creating an interesting pixel adventure world for players. In addition, many interesting gameplay and content elements have been added to the game. In the game, Heroes can:
[Barrage confrontation, show your godlike operations]
Hone your skills in battle and find your way in the dense barrage. Practice makes perfect for the true heroes!
[Group adventures, try the multiplayer battles model]
Find real friends in the game and enjoy the time of teaming up with friends. The true heroes are united as one.
[Collect equipment and match with unique builds]
The game currently contains 5 classes, 116 UR gears, 69 individual weapons, and 104 professional skills (it will be doubled in the future!). Try more powerful and unique build method. Everything is up to you. The true heroes are unique.
[Multiple gameplays, enjoy a brand-new experience]
Challenge the endless Hunting Grounds, exciting Arena, and the pursuit of cooperation in the Adventure which hones a variety of skills. The true heroes can do everything.
[Feed pets, start an unknown adventure]
Collect powerful pets with different characteristics which cast powerful skills, and accompany you in battle. The true heroes are never alone.
It is the time. The city of the heroes has opened. And the adventure is approaching, with unlimited possibilities.
